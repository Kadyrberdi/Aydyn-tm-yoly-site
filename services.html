Our Services

What We Can Do For You

Land Transport

Thanks to an extensive network of partners, we organize the delivery of goods by road to anywhere in the world. We have at our disposal all types of vehicles, including specialized vehicles for the transportation of certain types of cargo.

Ship Transport

Transportation of cargo by sea is much cheaper than air transportation. Ability to transport oversized, heavy and bulky cargo.

Air Transport

When speed is critical, we offer air freight services. We cooperate with leading airlines and airports, which allows us to provide fast and timely delivery.

Rail Transport

Our company offers all types of rail transportation, including container and groupage cargo. We provide fast and safe movement of goods throughout the country and beyond.

Multimodal Transport

This is an ideal solution for international logistics, when various modes of transport are used in the transportation process. We provide full control and coordination of all stages of transportation.

Transport by project

If you have a complex or large-scale project that requires competent logistics, our team of specialists will help develop and implement the most efficient transportation plan.
